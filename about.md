 * Lead by obeying
 * Propose, don’t impose
 * Represent, don’t replace
 * Anti-power Against power
 * Convince, don’t defeat
 * Everything for everyone, nothing for ourselves
 * Build, don’t destroy
 * We want a world where many worlds fit
